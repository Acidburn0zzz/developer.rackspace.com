Community & Outreach

Rackspace is committed to supporting communities. We offer small grants for workshops, hackdays, meetups, and conferences. We can also send Rackers to speak at your event and/or teach and mentor your group. Let us know what kind of support you need, and a Racker will reach out to you.

Please fill out and submit this form. We're already excited about your event!

@

What type of events does Rackspace sponsor?

We most frequently sponsor events that benefit developers and the broader tech community. Hackathons, user group meetings, conferences, and educational workshops (like Rails Girls) are all events we like to know about.

My event is next week. Can you help?

Because we receive so many requests, our team asks that you give us at least 3 weeks notice before your event. If you've missed it this year, we're happy to have a look next time.

What kind of sponsorships do you provide?

Depending on the event, it varies. Sometimes we are able to provide financial assistance. Sometimes we can help with an after party or drinkup. Sometimes we will make you cupcakes. It really depends on your circumstance, so let's talk about it.

Can we host our event in Rackspace's space?

It's possible! Select "Request use of Rackspace HQ" and tell us about your event. If your event is relevant to Rackspace, benefits the community, and fits with our schedule then we'll follow up to work out the details.

What about speakers?

So maybe you're set for sponsors, but you're looking for a speaker for your event. Let us know! We'd love to talk to you about that, too.

Still have questions about GitHub sponsorships? Drop our community team a line at sponsorships@rackspace.com
